Frequently Asked Questions

Can I bring a plus-one?

Unfortunately, NO. Our guests are limited and hand picked due to the limited capacity of the venue. We kindly request to not bring uninvited guests or tag-along companion, may they be an adult or kids.

Are children allowed at the party?

We love your little ones! However, we have decided to keep our birthday an adults-only event with the exception of immediate family and those requested. We encourage you to use this evening as a date night!

What time should I arrive?

The registration will be open at 4:00 pm and the program will start promptly at 5:00 pm. We encourage that you arrive 30 minutes before the program starts.

When is the RSVP deadline?

Kindly RSVP by January 19, 2025.

What if I still go even if I did not fill-out the RSVP?

Since your name is not listed, unfortunately, there will be no seat and no food available for you.

I replied “NO” on RSVP yet I changed my mind, Can I go?

It depends on the available slots. If you say “NO”, it means your slot will be given to another guest. Kindly inform the person in charge for more inquiries.
